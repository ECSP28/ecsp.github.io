Fine, let's get serious.

My online alias is ECSP, although you might also see me as ECSPdev or square guy

I am currently in kindergarten (i'm young) and ever since i played my first games back on the DS days, i've wanted to make games and to have people enjoy them.

The reason why i barely monetize my games is because i want to make them as accessible as possible, even if that means a net loss on my part.

I'm not into gamedev for the profits (okay, i am, but that's not the whole thing), i want people to actually enjoy what i create, even if that goal seems impossible, i'll do my best to make games amazing.
