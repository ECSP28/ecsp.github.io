I'm the square guy

Fine, let's get serious.

My online alias is ECSP, although you might also see me as ecsp dev or square guy

I am currently in kindergarten (young) and ever since i played my first games back on the DS days, i've wanted to make games and to have people enjoy them.

The reason why i barely monetize my games is because i want to make them as accessible as possible, even if that means a net loss on my part.

I live in Spain, i love my country

My ideas go deeper than digital strokes, i've created a universe with tragedy, adventure, fear and creativity. This is the universe of Square Runner, the universe of Boredom Inc., the universe of all the games i will ever make.

Absolute neutrality on everything; do not ask me pointless questions like ideology or rights.
