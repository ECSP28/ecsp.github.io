About

Welcome to the archipelago!

Square Runner tells the story of Bit, a Square alien
who wants to go back to his home planet.
Meet friends and foes, parkour through up to 80 levels
and learn about the world of Square Runner.

4 characters, 1 goal

Use a team of four characters to solve puzzles
and traverse through the areas of the
world. Each member specializes in its own
thing, but you must use all to complete your journey.

Levels packed with action!

Run through up to 80 levels filled with
gadgets, hazards, foes and tight jumps! This
is not an easy game by any means. Are you feeling
like taking on a challenge?

Make it back home!

Find leads for a way to get home and get
back to your planet! But are you going to forget the
the friends you made along the way once you get
home? Or will you stand by their side?
