Meet the team of wacky dudes that go fast and save the world

Bit

"The Square guy"

Bit is the fast one.
Unknown past, all it knows is that it fell from space into earth and wants to go home
Bit is the one you use to do a lot of platforming

  • Square creature (Alien)
  • Speed addict
  • Stayed exposed to the sun for too long; his skin bleached white
  • Fastest in the group

"The Robot guy"

D.O.M.

D.O.M. (Dome Operating Machine) is a little robot that can keep ecosystems inside of itself.
D.O.M. can be used to solve puzzles, open paths or for combat

  • Little robot
  • Grabs samples of ecosystems to use in combat or puzzle solving
  • Fragile
  • Extremely sarcastic
  • Prankster

Pickeffen

"The tank guy"

Pickeffen (Full name: Pickeffen Amadeus Von Mutum) is a TANK
He can withstand ANY attack thanks to its incredibly heavy shell, he can also push and move heavy objects, however, his shell stops him from being able to jump and move fast.
Pickeffen can be used for puzzle solving and progressing through areas with a lot of projectiles

  • Pinch creature (Also an alien)
  • Shell can withstand 7 elephants on top of it
  • Can move and carry heavy loads
  • Cute, innocent, smart and a fat ass
  • Scientist; the only one capable of piloting a spaceship

Soland

"The fish guy"

Soland is... a fish
If any god exists on this land, he wouldn't have let Salmonkind be a thing.
Leader of the local "Dragon Salmon" mafia, Soland manages weapon-dealing operations across his town of Salmonisburg
Soland is used for combat and sometimes, acrobatics.

  • "Fish" (Abomination, technically a salmon)
  • Local mafia lord, firearm dealer
  • Actually really dangerous individual
  • Has a gun on him at all times
  • Crackhead energy
